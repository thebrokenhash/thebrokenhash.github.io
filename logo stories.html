Media, Arts and Design Logo

It was the time when I was learning from the people and things I liked or loved. So, I decided to design a t- shirt for the council. For me I felt like some superhero doing stuffs (photography, design, fine arts, video making and what not) so I made the following (remember, as a t- shirt template).

Then when it was showed to fellow council members, somebody joked, "This can be our logo". On the other hand, I, to naive to understand the joke took that seriously. And worked on it to make a logo out of it. And made something like in inset.

One of my seniors, asked me to make it nicely, on a white sheet of paper and then they will see to it. I was hell excited, it was already, 1:00 AM of January 5 2013. But this excitement made me to do that right away (Though it took about an hour. I was afraid, that council people might change their mind and say that we do not need a logo or this logo). I faired it out. (I used a red ball point pen and it took me about an hour to make this)

Then after a few amendments by Nibodh, Shivam and rest of the team, logo was finalised. This stayed as the council's official logo for around 2 and a half years. Basic Philosophy was "We are SuperMAD, super artists of IITJ or the world may be"
