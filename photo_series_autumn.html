THE BROKEN HASH
I am Kirti Vardhan Rathore, 21 years old, an engineer with a knack for design, quite inspired to make good art. I do not claim to be a story teller but wish I could tell one. No matter how calm and composed a person's life seems, there's always a time in the past when they reached an impasse. A time when they went a little crazy. I guess people need that sort of stage in their lives, and I am in that phase of my life, and I do not know when it will get over.
