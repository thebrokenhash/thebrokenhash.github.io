FOREVER INFINITY





I love reading things, but I am not dedicated enough to read huge books, so I satisfy my desire by reading poetries, proses, some random texts on instagram, some movie dialogues etc. There were times when I cried after reading something, there were times, I laughed but when I tried to remember those words again, I failed. Definetely smething is wrong with me. I need to remember things that make me feel something. So we have a problem here, and the solution is simple, write them down or take a screenshot. But these solutions do not exite me.

Then comes the day, when I was reading somehting to quote it with a photo I clicked, and that text struck me hard. Luckily at that time I had some of my printed photographs on 3X3 card, so I wrote that text behind one of those cards.

More luckily, at that time I was reading Fountainhead so I used that card with photo on one side and text on other as a bookmark. And, Eureka!

I guess now you know that there are two things that I love to do, read and photograph. I not only love doing both these things but I want to do both of them as long as forever and as far as infinity. Plus I love printed photograps. So I decided to solve my problem using these bookmarks. Have a bundle of printed photographs and whenever you read something good write behind them. (Sounds Good)

I started doing that.




The name is Forever Infinity because both these things will be done as long as forever and as far as infinity.

Then there was a time when I was grateful of somebody, I thought of gifting them something (I was out of money, I am always out of money) So what I did was gifted them that bookmark (from here onwards referred to as FIcard) and fortunately it made them Happy.

From then onwards I incorporated this thing, when I am too grateful of the people in my life, I gift them FIcards. As a token of thank you and as a promise that I want that bond between us to last as long as forever and as far as infinity.

Let me know if you want one.
