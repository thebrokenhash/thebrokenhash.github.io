JoSH: Jodhpur Swachh

A first step in the direction of Swachh Bharat Abhiyan by Jodhpur Nagar Nigam. It will unfold in various stages. It focuses on increasing awareness among Jodhpurites regarding cleanliness of their surroundings using street plays and some very innovative campaigns.

Worked under the supervision of District Collector of Jodhpur, and designed the JoSH campaign.

Developed a set of five icons that will guide the citizens of Jodhpur what josh is all about. These icons were inspired by the simplicity of Traffic Signals. Like how efficiently they convey what they really want from you.

And Finally came the Mascot "Shreeman Josh".
